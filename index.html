About Eureka

Eureka, the Physics Society of Hindu College, is a platform for students passionate about the beauty and intricacies of physics. Our mission is to foster curiosity, ignite intellectual conversations, and empower members to explore the universe through the lens of science.

From organizing guest lectures by eminent physicists to conducting interactive workshops and quizzes, Eureka provides a holistic learning experience that extends beyond the classroom. We aim to bridge theoretical concepts with practical insights, encouraging innovation and collaboration.

Our Activities

Workshops

Guest Lectures

Talks by renowned physicists and educators to inspire and deepen our understanding of the universe.

Competitions

Exciting quizzes and debates to challenge your knowledge and test your critical thinking skills.
